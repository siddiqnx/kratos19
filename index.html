ABOUT KRATOS 19

KRATOS is the brainchild of the department of CSE in its efforts to bring a change in the way we view computer science.
Bright ideas and new concepts that reinforce and recalibrate the way in which we see and learn from the world .It is a long-standing tradition of our department to invite the budding minds of the student community to our grand event, Kratos’19.
Here you can code your way to victory, solve a crime, breakout from prison and much more. So join us on our quest, gear up and let the games begin.
